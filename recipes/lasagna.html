Classic Lasagna

Simple yet Powerful Lasagna

First made it's grand entrance in Naples, Italy during the Middle Ages in the 14th century. Lasagna is still loved by most and is fairly easy to make! These are the ingrediants and steps to making this wildly loved plate.

Ingrediants

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Tomato Paste
  • Onion
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer then bake
